ORV Manhwa Details

Omnisicent Reader's Viewpoint three main leads.

  • Kim Dokja
  • Yoo Joonhyuk
  • Han Sooyoung

Kim Dokja

He is the main protagonist of the story. He read a 3149 chapter light novel for the majority of his life and it suddenly came to his world causing disasters around the world. Now he is the only one in the world knowing everything about the story and he will do anything to see the conclusion. He doesn't follow the classic overpowered protagonist trope and is filled with flaws. He is a very enjoyable character to read about. Atributes and abilities:

  • reader
  • Omnisicent Reader's Viewpoint
  • bookmark

Yoo Joonghyuk

He is the protagonist of the light novel that Kim Dokja was reading. He is a regressor who relived the disasters countless of times. Each time his mental state gets worst and worst. However, this is only his "3rd" turn and you'll see how he and his only reader will interact. He may seem to have harsh personality at first glance, but the more you read about him you will see the reason for why he acts the way he does. Replaying and failing over and over does take a toll on the human mind. Atributes and abilities:

  • gamer
  • regressor
  • Sage's eye
  • he has around 30+ abilities from the start that I will not list. Just know that they are overpowered

Han Sooyoung

There is so much things I want to talk about this character but that will be a jungle of spoilers I have to avoid. So I will just tell you about her personality. She is a writer and she will do anything to stay alive in this disaster. She clashed with Dokja before they meet in the disaster and you will see how their relatioship grows as the story goes on. Very fun character definetely the best of the massive group (in my opinion). Atributes and abilities:

  • Author
  • Avatar
  • Lie detection
  • Poker face
